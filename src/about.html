Shipping Mediator

15 Years of Expertise in International Shipping

Buraq is a leading shipping mediator based in Istanbul, specializing in international cargo shipping, tracking, and shared container shipping. With 15 years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses and individuals worldwide.

Buraq's Specialized Shipping Services for Shipments to Palestine

Buraq offers dedicated shipping services to Palestine, providing reliable and efficient solutions for all your cargo transportation needs. With 15 years of experience in the industry, we have the expertise to handle your shipments with utmost care and professionalism.

Consistency and Reliability for Weekly Shipping

At Buraq, we pride ourselves on maintaining a consistent and reliable weekly shipping schedule. With 15 years of experience in the industry, we understand the importance of timely deliveries and strive to ensure that your cargo reaches its destination on time, every time.

Delivering Success Through Reliable International Shipping Solutions

At Buraq, we pride ourselves on our exceptional track record of providing efficient and reliable international shipping services. With over 15 years of experience, we have successfully delivered shipments to destinations worldwide, ensuring customer satisfaction every step of the way.
